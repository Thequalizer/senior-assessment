Modernization while keeping business delivery moving: reduce complexity, raise quality, and make the platform easier to scale.

AEM 6.5 React Headless Governance

Context

The platform needed a pragmatic path from legacy AEM component implementations to a modern React-based delivery model—without disrupting release cadence or stakeholder confidence.

Goal

  • Ship user-facing improvements while reducing long-term maintenance cost.
  • Improve predictability and reduce regressions through better structure and quality controls.
  • Create a repeatable migration pattern other engineers can follow.

My Role

  • Technical ownership: architecture decisions and standards.
  • Delivery leadership: planning, reviews, unblocking, aligning distributed teams.
  • Quality bar: accessibility, performance, and observability mindset.

Key Contributions

  • Defined migration approach and component patterns (what, why, and how).
  • Helped map dependencies and risk areas to make migration safer and clearer.
  • Improved developer experience through clearer structure and practical tooling.
